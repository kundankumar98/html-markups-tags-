Numerical Aperture is the measure of the ability of an optical fiber to collect or confine the incident light ray inside it.
Numerical Aperture is defined as the Sine of half of the angle of fibre's light acceptance cone. i.e. NA= Sin θ where θ, is called acceptance cone angle.

Ilha da Queimada Grande, also known as Snake Island, is an island off the coast of Brazil in the Atlantic Ocean. It is administered as part of the municipality of Itanhaém in the State of São Paulo. The island is small in size, only 43 hectares (106 acres), and has a temperate climate. The island's terrain varies considerably, ranging from bare rock to rainforest. It is the only home of the critically endangered, venomous Bothrops insularis (golden lancehead pit viper), which has a diet of birds. The snakes became trapped on the island when rising sea levels covered up the land that connected it to the mainland. The ensuing selection pressure allowed the snakes to adapt to their new environment, increasing rapidly in population and rendering the island dangerous to public visitation. Queimada Grande is closed to the public in order to protect both people and the snake population; access is only available to the Brazilian Navy and selected researchers vetted by the Chico Mendes Institute for Biodiversity Conservation, the Brazilian federal conservation unit.

NOTE:-

action:-
Every 'form' element requires an action attribute. Its value is the URL for the page on the server that will receive the information in the form when it is submitted.(Here form element used is actually useless).

method:-
Forms can be sent using one of two methods: get or post.
--> With the get method, the values from the form are added to the end of the URL specified in the action attribute. Get method is ideal for:-
● short forms (such as search boxes)
● when you are just retrieving data from the web server (not sending information that should be added to or deleted from a database)
--> With the post method the values are sent in what are known as HTTP headers. As a rule of thumb you should use the post method if your form:-
● allows users to upload a file
● is very long. (long forms)
● contains sensitive data (e.g. passwords)
● adds information to, or deletes information from, a database
If the method attribute is not used, the form data will be sent using the get method.
