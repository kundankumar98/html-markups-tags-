In HTML, we use two types of tages (markups):-
Structural:- the elements that you can use to describe both headings and paragraphs.
Semantic:- which provides extra information; such as where emphasis is placed in a sentence, that something you have written is a quotation (and who said it), the meaning of acronyms, and so on.

Optical fibre:-
a thin flexible fibre with a glass core through which light signals can be sent with very little loss of strength.
Numerical aperture:-
Numerical Aperture is the measure of the ability of an optical fiber to collect or confine the incident light ray inside it.
Numerical Aperture is defined as the Sine of half of the angle of fibre's light acceptance cone. i.e. NA= Sin θ where θ, is called acceptance cone angle.
